Fast Food

Fast Food Every Day

Warm-up: Do you eat fast food? How many times a week?
Fact: In the US, 1 in 3 adults eats fast food every day.
  1. Do you eat fast food? How many times a week?
  2. Is McDonald's or KFC popular in your country too?
  3. What is your favorite fast food?
  4. Is fast food cheap or expensive in your country?
Key words
every day favorite
2
Healthy Food

Salad or Fries?

Warm-up: Do you eat salad? How often?
Fact: Now, many US restaurants have healthy food too, like salad or a wrap.
  1. Do you like salad?
  2. Is healthy food expensive in your country?
  3. Do you cook at home, or eat at a restaurant more?
  4. What is a healthy food from your country?
Key words
salad wrap cook
3
Prices

Is Food Expensive?

Warm-up: Is food expensive in your country?
Fact: In the US, food at the supermarket and at restaurants can be expensive.
  1. Is food expensive or cheap in your country?
  2. Where do you buy food: supermarket or small stores?
  3. Is it expensive to eat at a restaurant in your country?
  4. Do you save money when you cook at home?
Key words
groceries save money
4
Compare Cultures

Same Food, Different Country

Warm-up: Is there a McDonald's in your city?
Fact: American fast food restaurants are now in many countries around the world.
  1. Do you have American fast food restaurants in your country?
  2. Do people in your country eat fast food often?
  3. Is fast food a big part of your culture?
  4. What food is famous in your country?
Key words
around the world common
