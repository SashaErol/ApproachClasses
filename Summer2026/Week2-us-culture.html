Shopping

🛒 Big Stores, Big Packs

Warm-up: Do you know Costco or BJ's? What do people buy there?
  1. In the US, people buy food in bulk at stores like Costco. Do you do this in your country?
  2. You need a membership card to shop at Costco. Is that normal in your country?
  3. Is there a big warehouse store in your city? What is it called?
  4. Do you think buying in bulk saves money? Or does it make people waste more food?
Key words
wholesale discount stock up
2
Restaurants

🥡 Can I Get a Doggy Bag?

Warm-up: What do you do with food you don't finish at a restaurant?
  1. In the US, people often ask for a doggy bag for their leftovers. Do people do this in your country?
  2. Do you feel embarrassed asking for a box, or is it normal?
  3. What do you call this "to-go" box in your language?
  4. Why do you think Americans ask for doggy bags so often?
Key words
to-go box portion waste
3
Health & Body

🏃 Fit Nation or Fast-Food Nation?

Warm-up: When you think of an American, what body type comes to mind? Why?
  1. Are people in your country mostly fit, or mostly overweight?
  2. Is "all Americans are fat" a true fact or just a stereotype?
  3. Do people in your country exercise a lot? Where do they exercise?
  4. What food habits are different between the US and your country?
Key words
obesity habit portion size
4
Laws

⚖️ Buying a Gun: Easy or Hard?

Warm-up: Can a regular person buy a gun in your country? Is it easy?
  1. In your country, is it easy or hard to buy a gun?
  2. Fact: gun laws are NOT the same in every US state. Does this surprise you?
  3. Do you think every country should have the same gun laws?
  4. Have you ever seen a real gun in daily life? Where?
Key words
background check allowed strict
5
Identity

🎌 Hand on Heart: Patriotism

Warm-up: Do you know all the words to your national anthem? Try to sing one line!
  1. Are people in your country patriotic? How do they show it?
  2. Do you know your national anthem by heart?
  3. Do you see the flag often — at school, at home, on TV?
  4. Do you think Americans are more or less patriotic than people in your country?
Key words
pledge pride tradition
